About The New Alliance Party

Learn a little more about the type of political group that we are.



A little about us
Hello! We're the New Alliance party and we want to make America suitable for everyone again. To ensure that this happens, we have six immident problems that we would like to address including the War on Terror, Immigration, Gun Control, Marijuana laws, Job outsourcing and Abortion. We want the American people to feel safe and that the government doesn't have too much power and is trying to control everything. The New Alliance Party wants what is best for not only America itself, but all of the American citizens. We will listen to you. We will agree or negotiate with you.
Our color
We chose the color brown for our political group because brown represents stability and support, something that the New Alliance Party aims to give to Americans. We also want our government to be a little more independent and more dependable for the American people.
Our symbol
We feel that the eagle represents freedom, which is why we chose it to be our logo. The eagle also represents power and vision, something that we would like the United States government to have. We want power in the sence of being able to have a strong military, but not neccessarly using it. We also have a vision, a vision of how America will look after we get elected into office.
