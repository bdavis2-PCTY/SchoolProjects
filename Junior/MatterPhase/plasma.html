PLASMA




When enough energy is added to gas, it is Ionized into Plasma. Plasma is similiar to gas, yet very different. Plasma is something that most people don't actually get to see up close, it doesn't naturally exist on Earth. In plasma, the positively and negatively charged particles are separated. Even though natural, pure plasma isn't on Earth, there are a lot of man-made plasmas. This includes fluorescent light bulbs, and in neon gas.

When energy is taken away from plasma, it will deionize back into a gas.
