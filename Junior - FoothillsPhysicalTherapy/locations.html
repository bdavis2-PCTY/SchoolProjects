Boise Clinic

We are located at 1673 W. Shoreline Dr, Suite 230, in the Riverwalk Center along the Boise River. Our office is convenient to Downtown, the Boise Bench, Garden City, and Boise State University. And, because we are directly off the Connector at the River Street exit, we are within just a few minutes of Meridian and West Boise!

 

From I-84 and I-184:

  1. Take I-184 to the River Street Exit.
  2. Take a right on S. Americana Blvd.
  3. Take a right on Shoreline Drive.
  4. Take an immediate left turn into the parking lot.

 

From State Street:

  1. Turn south on N 16th Street.
  2. 16th Street turns into S. Americana Blvd.
  3. Take a right on Shoreline Drive.
  4. Take an immediate left turn into the parking lot

Meridian Clinic

We are located at 1618 S Millennium Way on the southeast corner of Overland Rd. and Millennium Way in the Ada West Professional Building (directly across the street from the Majestic Movie Theater). We have easy access right off the Interstate from either the Meridian Rd. Exit or the Eagle Rd Exit.

 

Please contact us if you need additional directions.
